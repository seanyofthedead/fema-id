## Classification definitions

| Classification | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Confirmed Requirement** | Stated directly in the transcript as something the solution must do, or as a factual constraint the solution must respect. |
| **Strong Inference** | Not stated as a formal requirement, but clearly implied by the discussion (e.g., an approach proposed and positively received, or a fact that logically necessitates a capability). |
| **Assumption** | Mentioned with explicit uncertainty in the transcript, or required for the demo but not evidenced; carries an `ASSUMP-` entry in file 03. |
| **Future Enhancement** | Discussed as beyond the demo / later-phase value. |
| **Out of Scope** | Explicitly handled elsewhere or excluded. |

**Confidence** reflects how certain we are that the row states what the client actually needs (H = direct, unhedged transcript statement; M = hedged/approximate statement or inference; L = weakly supported).
