The Morning Brief

Program-integrity briefing

Federal payment-integrity law (PIIA — the Payment Integrity Information Act) requires FEMA to know each reportable program’s actual disbursements by fiscal year and to run a Preliminary Risk Assessment (PRA) per program — “preliminary” because it is the start of a lifecycle, not a terminal status. This brief reads the fiscal-year extract for you and leads with what needs your decision.
