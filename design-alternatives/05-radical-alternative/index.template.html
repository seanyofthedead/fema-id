Reading & keyboard guide

SYNTHETIC-DEMO. Every number on this page is synthetic demonstration data, computed live in this file from an embedded synthetic extract (1,459 transaction rows, FY2022–FY2026). Nothing here is a FEMA record. The dataset is calibrated to public obligation envelopes; program names and disaster declaration (DR) numbers are real public identifiers.

“AI” capabilities shown (mapping suggestions, drafted assessment answers) are simulated and deterministic: the machine proposes and explains; it never owns a reportable number, and nothing finalizes without human sign-off. Historical assessment milestones before FY2026 are illustrative — real records exist at FEMA from 2018–19 onward. Glossary: PRA = Preliminary Risk Assessment; PIIA = Payment Integrity Information Act; YoY = year over year; DR = major disaster declaration number; TAFS = Treasury Appropriation Fund Symbol; FY = federal fiscal year (Oct 1 – Sep 30).
