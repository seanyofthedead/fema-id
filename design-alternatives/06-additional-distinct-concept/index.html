Synthetic-data disclosure

Every figure in this demonstration is synthetic (SYNTHETIC-DEMO), calibrated to public obligation envelopes. Program names and disaster-declaration (DR) numbers are real public identifiers; amounts, transactions, codes and TAFS (Treasury Appropriation Fund Symbol) values are stand-ins. Every export is watermarked.

Simulated capabilities

Rule mining, similarity suggestions, identities/signatures, exports, and the fuller-program-list preview are simulated for demonstration. Deterministic code computes every reportable number live from the embedded extract; the simulated "AI" only proposes, explains and scores — it never owns a number. Mapping confidence below 0.85 routes to the exception queue.

Glossary

PIIA — Payment Integrity Information Act. PRA — Preliminary Risk Assessment ("preliminary" is a starting point in a lifecycle, not a terminal status). YoY — year over year. TAFS — Treasury Appropriation Fund Symbol. DR — major disaster declaration number. Fiscal year (FY) runs Oct 1 – Sep 30. Disbursements are payments made — never presented here as obligations.
